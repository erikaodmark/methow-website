Our Headquarters

Tucked in the Town of Twisp lies the oasis Methow Valley Inn.

The historic Methow Valley Inn opened their doors in 1912. Along the way, the Inn became an integral part of Twisp’s rich history. The Methow Valley Inn has been lovingly restored as a nightly rental B&B and group rental.

Methow River Research and Education Center’s has established it's Foundation's Headquarters at the Methow Valley Inn. The Goal is to provide a permanent home base for the Foundation to work from. The headquarters is a resource information hub, library, presentation center, will host events and guest speakers and has rooms for presenters.

The Inn is only 15 minutes from Winthrop and 30 minutes from the town of Mazama. Other environmental groups are in close proximity.

For more information about the history of the Methow Valley Inn go to the web site at: methowvalleyinn.com
