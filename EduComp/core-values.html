An Introduction

The Methow River Ranch is a unique/special slice of land located on both sides of the Methow River right after a ( ). We have an opportunity to...

Create a new system for interactive monitoring and at the same time, educate kids on how to run and process data. They will learn about their reality- the environment surrounding them. They will also learn about being a professional.

Above the river front we have an alfalfa field that is being irrigated by ( ). This is increasing the water output into the river which benefits the salmon and the underwater ecosystem. We have a side channel composed of a series of ponds that serve as a water filter. The ponds enrich nutrients into the water. This water cycles into the Methow tributary. We also have beavers located on the ranch. The structures that the beavers create cool the water. Cooler waters helps the salmon and provides structure/housing for salmon as well. What we need is a monitoring system. We would like to take that data and assimilate it in a useful way. This can serve as a template for other conservation groups to learn things.
