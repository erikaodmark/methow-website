Our Mission To reach out to landowners, farmers, educators, and community members of the Methow River Valley for their help in identifying problems and shaping solutions for future generations.

Who We Are

We are a dedicated group of various backgrounds, students, educators, farmers, ranchers, who study the environment and work the land in the Methow Valley.

We seek to engage and work with the people of the valley, and provide them with relevant information to help in their everyday lives for the health of the valley, its people, and resources for generations to come.


  • Brings together community
  • Enhances education
  • Drives research
  • Raises environmental awareness
